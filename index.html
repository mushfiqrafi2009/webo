    Faridganj A. R Pilot Model Government High School is a traditional educational institution in Faridganj Upazila, Chandpur District, Chittagong Division, Bangladesh. School is located in the middle market of Faridganj Upazila and the municipal town. It is the first digitalized school in Faridganj Upazila. In front of it is a huge playground and in the back is one of the main rivers of Faridganj Upazila, the Dakatia River. It was established in 1946 by Khan Bahadur Abidur Reza Chowdhury. The school was named after the founder. It was a boys school in the time of establishment but later been transformed to combined school having students of both genders. This institution was nationalized on September 13, 2018. The schools offers education for 6th grade to 10th grade.
    The infrastructure of the school evolved over the years. Initially there was only one building of the school. Later 2 different buidings were built namely the Academic Building and the Technology Building. And also another small building was made for the library and the laboratory.
